Natural Stat Trick Refresh Centre

Checking
  1. Download the three exports.
    1 · Team data2 · Skater data3 · Goalie data
  2. Choose the three downloaded CSV files.
  3. Prepare one refresh file.

    The tracker validates and combines the exports on your device. Nothing is sent anywhere at this stage.

  4. Upload that one file.

    On GitHub, choose naturalstattrick-refresh.json, then press Commit changes. The automatic deployment does the rest.

    Upload refresh file to GitHub

Data coverage

Automatic season rollover

The tracker checks for the NHL's next regular-season schedule on every update. It switches only after a substantial schedule is published, keeps every completed season in the Season menu, and clearly marks analytics that are still awaiting a new-season release.

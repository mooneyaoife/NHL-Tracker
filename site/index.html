      The tracker recognises each export automatically—the filenames and selection order do not matter.

      The files are validated and combined entirely on your device.

      Select the three CSV files above.

      On GitHub, choose naturalstattrick-refresh.json, then press Commit changes. The site updates automatically.

    Automatic season rollover

    The tracker checks for the NHL's next regular-season schedule on every update. It switches only after a substantial schedule is published, keeps every completed season in the Season menu, and clearly marks analytics that are still awaiting a new-season release.
